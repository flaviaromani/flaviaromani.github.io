MARA...VILLA

«Mara» was the nickname given to Frida Kahlo by her lover, the catalan painter José Bartoli. His inspiration came from hindu mythology, where Mara is the Goddess of desire, temptation and seduction. Frida added «villa» to her nickname, in order to make others believe that «Mara» was the abrreviation of «Maravilla» (wonder).
