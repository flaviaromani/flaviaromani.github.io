VICEVERSA MAGAZINE - LOGO

[ co-founder | creative director ]

ViceVersa Magazine is an online weekly publication (in Spanish) based in NYC. Its mission is to promote Latin American talent and excellence that exists in the United States in all its forms: cultural, social, economic, political, and scientific.


PROFILES

[ art director | illustrator ]

The profile pictures of ViceVersa Magazine's 300+ collaborators are stylized black and white illustrations. These illustrations have become a trademark of the magazine.


#SOYVICEVERSA

[ art director | illustrator ]

The slogan #SoyViceVersa was created for the first anniversary of the magazine. Illustrations of famous latino personalities (Frida Kahlo, Julio Cortázar, Salvador Dalí, Jorge Luis Borges, Gabriel García Márquez) were drawn for this occasion. Using the same illustration style as for the profile pictures of ViceVersa's collaborators, the intent was to draw a visual parallel between the ViceVersa Magazine’s community and these inspiring Latin American artists and writers.


PROMOTIONAL VIDEOS

[ art director | video editor ]

Each week, ViceVersa Magazine publishes two interviews: the first is of a well-known Latin American personality, and the second of an emerging Latin American talent. These interviews are introduced by experimental short videos, which set the tone for the articles.


LAUNCHING CAMPAIGN VIDEOS

[ art director | video editor ]

Viceversa Magazine was launched on August 10th, 2014, coinciding with a super moon in the sky. This coincidence inspired the promotional videos leading to the launch of the magazine.
